Industries

  • Aerospace

    Since the 1950s, we have supplied critical seals and polymer parts to the aerospace industry for commercial/military aircraft and launch vehicles. Our OmniSeal®, Rulon® & Meldin® products traveled millions of miles on the Space Shuttle and Mars Rover Curiosity, and are forming the future with the Space Launch System and innovative aircraft technology.

     » Learn More

  • Automotive

    Partnering with L+S, we acquired expertise in injection-molded seal rings and thrust washers for powertrain & transmissions, building on more than 40 years of experience with OEMs, Tier 1 and Tier 2 companies. We strive to reduce friction, leakage, wear, noise, and emission; and contribute to energy-efficient solutions with our OmniSeal® polymer seals and Meldin® thermoplastic products.

     » Learn More

  • Energy

    Our PTFE sealing and polymer solutions were introduced to the Oil and Gas sector in the 1970s. Since then, our OmniSeal® spring-energized and rotary lip seals have addressed critical issues including aggressive media, sour gas environment, resistance to rapid gas decompression and extreme cryogenic operating conditions, leading to the next generation of materials that are NORSOK and API certified.

     » Learn More

  • Life Sciences

    Nurturing over 30 years of relationships with medical, dental, analytical equipment & pharmaceutical customers, we are a trusted design and manufacturing partner due to our high purity OmniSeal® spring-energized and rotary lip seals, Rulon® PTFE bearings and Meldin® thermoset polyimide parts, providing a precise level of sealing and meeting stringent cleanroom requirements.

     » Learn More

  • Industrial

    Our OmniSeal® spring-energized and rotary lip seals, Rulon® PTFE bearings and Meldin® thermoset polyimide products are used in compressors, pumps, valves, commercial machinery & food industry equipment where it is critical to be contaminant free, withstand aggressive media and handle extreme pressures/temperatures. We ensure our customers a lifetime of confidence with maintenance free equipment.

Onshore Drilling, Production & Transport

Our sealing solutions are compliant with API 6A and fugitive emission standards ISO 15848 and SHELL MESC SPE 77-300 for valves in the upstream and downstream sectors.

Subsea Drilling, Production & Transport

We support customer demand for high-performance, subsea equipment sealing solutions compliant with API 17D, addressing extreme pressure and temperature issues.

Cryogenic Production & Transport

Where harsh chemicals and liquefied gasses (LNG and LPG) are present and need to be handled properly, our customers rely on our sealing systems for protection, reliability and lower costs.

Floating Production

A key technology using large and complex turret swivel stacks. Our OmniSeal® seals with back-up rings handle larger diameter sealing systems with ease.

Renewable Energy

We are committed to working with renewable energy technology that is affordable and sustainable such as our OmniSeal® windmill gearbox seals.

Life Sciences

About Saint-Gobain

Saint-Gobain designs, manufactures and distributes materials and solutions for the construction, mobility, healthcare and other industrial application markets; they can be found everywhere in our living places and daily life, providing wellbeing, performance and safety. 

Saint-Gobain Seals' polymer and metal seals; composites; PTFE components; thermoset polyimides and thermoplastics are used globally in critical systems that "move" us every day and into the future: aviation, space, life science, industrial and energy.
